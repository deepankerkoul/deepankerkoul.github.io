Overview

While I have harboured the dream of writing a work of fiction since I read my first novel, I have mostly hid into the comfort zone of reality. I find it easier to talk about the world we see all around us than make up new ones, especially when we know so little.

Here you can find my articles as well as stories that I have tried to spin. I have some reflective pieces as well as my take on certain policy decisions. You can also take a look at some of the book summaries.
